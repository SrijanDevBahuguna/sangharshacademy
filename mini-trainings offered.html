BEGINNER

At this level we start with the early stages of the individual by giving him knowledge about the sport ,it rules and make sure that he is well aware of them till the end of the training. after this we work on his fitness level and perform regular medical tests to see whether the given training is benificial for him or not.

INTERMEDIATE

As the individual has been through the beginner level, he/she goes through this intermediate level where training becomes a little more tough. But because of this, the individual enhances its mental and physical strength and strives to become the best. The individual develops a passion for the game and is in the first stage to become a professional.

ADVANCED

In the advanced stage, the training becomes more harder but the individual is ready for that type of training. In this stage we send the individuals for various trials that are organised at various levels such as district, state etc. to see there performance apart from training.
